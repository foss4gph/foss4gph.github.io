About FOSS4G PH

Since 2011, FOSS4G Philippines had been conducting workshops and talks to promote the use of free and open source technologies for geospatial applications.

FOSS4G PH TALKS 2017

FOSS4G Philippines will conduct a series of talks on May 24, 2017 as a pre-conference event for the Philippine Geomatics Symposium 2017 - “Geospatial Digital Data for Development (Geospatial 3D): Acquisition, Modeling, Analysis, Visualization, and Applications of 3D Data”.
